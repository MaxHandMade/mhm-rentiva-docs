Admins can now generate and email a payable link for a booking's remaining amount, and full-payment-only bookings no longer get mislabeled "Deposit" in a mixed cart.

<!--truncate-->

## Feature

- **Admins can now generate a payable link for a booking's remaining amount directly from the "Deposit Management" box on the booking edit screen, and email it to the customer with one click.** The link is also shown on-screen for copying (WhatsApp, SMS, phone), and works even for customers without a site account.

## Fix

- **Bookings whose payment is full-payment-only (e.g. a VIP transfer configured without deposit support) no longer get mislabeled "Deposit" when the checkout's cart-wide payment-type selector is set to "Deposit Payment" for a mixed cart.** The amount charged was always correct; only the internal label is corrected.
