Two additions to Vendor Management: a one-click Unsuspend action for suspended vendors, and a Rate History table in the Commission tab showing the full audit trail of platform-wide commission policies.

<!--truncate-->

## What's New

### Vendor Unsuspend Action

Admins can now **unsuspend a vendor directly** from the Vendor Management admin page — no need to navigate to the user profile. When a vendor is unsuspended:

1. The `rentiva_vendor` role is restored.
2. Vehicles that were moved to *Pending Review* at suspension time are re-published.
3. The action is logged in the commission audit trail.

### Commission Rate History

The Commission tab now displays a **Rate History table** showing all historical platform-wide commission policies in reverse chronological order. Each entry records:

- Effective date
- Rate (%)
- Admin-supplied label (e.g. "Q1 2026 standard rate")

The history is append-only — rates are never deleted or overwritten.

### Menu Grouping Update

Vendor menus (Vendor Management, Vendor Reports, Payout Requests) now consistently appear at the bottom of the admin sidebar via a `priority-15` hook across all contexts.
