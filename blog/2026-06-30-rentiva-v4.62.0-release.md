When a Pro license expires, catalog items added during the Pro period that exceed the Lite plan limits — vehicles, add-on services, and transfer routes — are now automatically hidden from the public site instead of staying live. Data is never deleted; everything is restored the moment you upgrade to Pro again.

<!--truncate-->

## What's New

### Lite Limit Enforcement on Downgrade

- **Over-limit items hidden, not deleted.** Catalog items added during Pro (vehicles, add-on services, transfer routes) that exceed the Lite limit are hidden from the public site when a license expires. Data is preserved in full and restored automatically when you upgrade to Pro again.
- **"Lite limit — hidden" badge in admin lists.** Admin vehicle, add-on service, and transfer route lists mark each over-limit item with a clear badge so you can see at a glance which items are currently hidden from visitors.
- **Vehicle limit notice shows hidden count.** The vehicle limit notice in the admin dashboard now indicates how many vehicles are currently hidden due to the Lite limit, in addition to the total count.
- **Hidden vehicles cannot be booked.** Over-limit vehicles that are hidden from the public site can no longer be booked via the front end. Existing confirmed bookings for those vehicles are unaffected.

## Fixes

- The **Vendor Payout Requests** admin menu item no longer appears when running the Lite version of the plugin.
