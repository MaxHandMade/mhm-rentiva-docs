The Customer Management tab is where you configure the automated communication rules for users who register or make bookings in your system. Access it via **MHM Rentiva > Settings > Customer Management**.

---

## 📧 Customer Communication Settings

Manage email notifications here to enhance the user experience and keep your customers informed throughout the process.

- **Send Welcome Email:** Sends a brand-customized "Welcome" message to a new user who registers via MHM Rentiva forms.
- **Send Booking Notifications:** Sends the customer an instant update whenever a booking status changes (e.g., Pending → Confirmed or Cancelled).
