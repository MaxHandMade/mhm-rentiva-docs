Vehicle Categories let you divide the vehicles in your fleet into logical groups and provide powerful filtering options on the frontend. Managed via **MHM Rentiva > Vehicle Categories**.

On this page you can sort your vehicles into main classes and create a hierarchical structure.

---

## Category Management (Categories)

Used to divide your vehicles into main classes (e.g. Economy, VIP, SUV, Commercial).

- **Hierarchical Structure:** Categories can be nested (e.g. Passenger > Sedan).
- **Category Image:** In some themes, category icons or images are used on listing pages.
- **Shortcode Integration:** Use parameters such as `[rentiva_vehicles category="vip"]` to list vehicles in a specific category.

## Filtering and Search Compatibility

Every category you define here automatically becomes a filter option in the frontend vehicle search form. Your customers can easily find vehicles in a specific category thanks to these definitions.

### Section Summary
- Segment your vehicles with **Categories**.
- Keep complex fleets organized using **Hierarchy** (e.g. Passenger > Sedan > Luxury Sedan).
- **URL Structure:** Make category-specific archive pages SEO-friendly.
